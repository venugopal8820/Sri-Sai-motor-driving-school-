About Sri Sai Motor Driving School

Purpose & Planning

Our mission is to create safe, confident drivers through a practical curriculum: classroom theory, real-road practice, mock tests and defensive driving. We plan lessons for progressive skill-building, ensuring every student masters vehicle control, traffic rules, and safe decision-making.

Target Audience

  • First-time drivers (teens & young adults)
  • Working professionals with busy schedules
  • People preparing for the driving license test
  • Drivers seeking refresher or defensive driving courses

Vehicles & Safety

All training vehicles are regularly serviced and fitted with required safety controls. Our instructors are certified and experienced in coaching nervous or first-time drivers with patience and clarity.
